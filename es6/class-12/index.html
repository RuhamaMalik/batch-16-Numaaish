Classic Margherita Pizza

Timings: 15

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

  • Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C)
  • Roll out the pizza dough and spread tomato sauce evenly.
  • Top with slices of fresh mozzarella and fresh basil leaves.
